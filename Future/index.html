Dark

The future is always bright.

No one knows what it holds.

The past is behind you, why dwell on it?

They say nothing is set in stone, but the past is done and gone. You can't go back and change it. If you could, would you? You change that one thing that has always bothered you. Wouldn't it be nice just to go back? Back, back, back. Go back in time to simpler days. When you only worried about childish things. Time is fleeting, life is short. Yet, isn't life the longest thing we get? There is comfort in the past and all the nostalgia it holds. Learn from the past, what happened has shaped you. Would you be the same if it did not happen? Everything that has happened, whether fate or not, is done and gone. Everyone has something they would love to take back, but at what cost? Change one thing and you could lose everything. It would be so easy if you could click to go back.
