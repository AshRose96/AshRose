Mountain Series: Watercolor

Watercolor Pigment, Cotton Based Paper, India Ink, 2020

While working on this series, my main concern was keeping each scene unique. Each is made to have its own atmosphere while potentially existing in the same spcae. I would like my viewers to wonder about the story behind each image. The largest of the paintings is 15"x22" with a total of 7 works.
