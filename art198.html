BFA Show Piece

This piece will feature at least one digital painting i made paired with a rendered and animated scene. Both will depict the same scene, one will be a still image while the other one moves. The biggest difficulty I have faced so far is understanding and using the Augnemted Reality tools to achieve my goal. At first I tried using the Vuforia through Unity, but my image was not working corectly as a marker. Now I have been exploring using a-Frame and the possibility of incorporating the barcodes into the piece. At the moment, the I am figuring out how to import my own models for use.
